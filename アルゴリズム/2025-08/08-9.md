### [paizaスキルチェック「C139:誤発注」](https://paiza.jp/challenges/share/CjTLkMGX71mf48vYIlWlAe0cLpLNkpDfUpDp-MUOeMc?source=social)
#### 自身の解答

```ruby
n = gets.to_i
orders = []

n.times do
    orders << gets.to_i
end

order_n = 0
n.times do |number|
    unless orders.include?(number + 1)
        order_n += 1
    end
end

puts order_n
```

### [paizaスキルチェック「C170:目標ポイント」](https://paiza.jp/challenges/share/9kkoet7dSVxLs2VXREV5KzbRwHNnDsX2-RHxdlaF6VY?source=social)
#### 自身の解答
```ruby
n, target_point = gets.split.map(&:to_i)

bills = gets.split.map(&:to_i)
current_point = 0

bills.each do |bill|
    current_point += bill / 100
end

if target_point >= current_point
    puts (target_point - current_point) * 100
else
    puts 0
end
```
